### Vehicle Upgrade Framework 2.0
***
##### Currently supported functionalities.
***
* Health  `health="Int"`
* Defense `defense="Int"`
* Mine `mine_fuel & mine_speed & mine ="Int"`
* Speed `speed="Int"`
* Weight `maxe_weight="Int"`
* HitDamage `hitDamage="Int"`
* Cloaking `hide="Bool"` and
> ```xml
<handleSecond>
<action>updateCloak(object)</action>
</handleSecond>
```
